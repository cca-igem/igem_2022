SchistoGONE

Novel Prevention of Parasitic Diseases•CCA iGEM 2022

The disease is caused by the parasite Schistosoma mansoni, which invades freshwater snails as an intermediate host. Once it is grown, the parasite infects humans by penetration through the skin.

Currently, only one primary treatment for this disease exists—praziquantel. Unfortunately, due to a lack of accessible healthcare in third-world countries, it often reaches schistosomiasis patients too late.
